Moisture Meter for Cashew Processing

A purpose-built, field-ready moisture meter designed for measuring moisture in Raw Cashew Nuts (RCN) and Natural Wholes (NW). Basically: a device so processors don’t have to guess moisture using wisdom, intuition, or feelings.

Somehow, despite many prototypes that almost made us give up, this device is now used in 100+ processing units across India and Africa. We’re still surprised too.

The short version

A cashew-specific moisture meter built for actual factories — stable readings, repeatable results, and simple enough to use without a manual (or calling an engineer every time). Not for roasted or broken grades — we’re not magicians.

• Built under IXE Tronics Pvt. Ltd.
• Designed using real field feedback & real mistakes

The problem

Moisture affects cutting quality, yield, roasting, and storage. But most processors rely on “experience”, gut feeling, or grain meters that guess worse than the weather forecast. It works sometimes… until it doesn’t.

  • No tool built specifically for cashews
  • Grain meters give unstable readings and emotional roller-coasters
  • Lab testing is accurate but slower than the drying process itself

What we built

A moisture meter specifically for RCN and Natural Whole kernels. Portable, repeatable, and designed to withstand factory conditions including dust, dropped tools, and occasionally frustrated operators.

  • Simple operation (even for people who absolutely refuse to read manuals)
  • Stable readings across batches
  • Survives dust, rough handling, and voltage that fluctuates like exam results
  • Affordable and repairable (because things will break eventually)

How it works (the non-dramatic version)

1. Sampling

Put a fixed quantity of RCN or NW in the chamber. No rituals required.

2. Measurement

Electrical response correlates with moisture — physics doing its thing.

3. Reading & decisions

Compare trays & batches, decide when to dry or cut — with fewer arguments.

Where it is used

Use-cases

  • RCN drying & steaming QC
  • Natural Wholes before roasting or cutting
  • Tray-wise & batch-wise consistency checks
  • Checking storage readiness (aka mould prevention)

Design priorities

  • Optimized for RCN & NW only (no all-in-one magical box nonsense)
  • Portable, rugged and repeatable
  • Repairable — not a black box mystery device
  • Works for small, medium, and giant factories alike

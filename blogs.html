Blogs

This page collects the different things I’m writing as I attempt to understand embedded systems, TinyML, signal processing, mathematics, and teaching. These are working notes — expect updates, corrections, and the occasional “why did I write this?” moment.

At the top you’ll find blog series — the topics I got obsessed with long enough to write multiple posts about. Below are individual blogs: solo pieces wandering around freely looking for a series to join someday.

A small confession: while I do the thinking, experimenting, and face-planting required to understand these topics, I happily outsource the heavy lifting of reorganising my chaos to multiple LLMs. I wrestle with the concepts, equations and hardware problems; they help fix my grammar, untangle my rambling thoughts, and occasionally tell me I’m wrong. If something here looks neat and structured that was probably them. If something looks confusing that was definitely me.

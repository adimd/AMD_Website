Hi, I’m Adithya. This site has two main halves: the projects I’m building in embedded systems, sensing and TinyML, and the learning notes where I try to really understand the math, ML and DSP behind them. Why this site exists

I’ve noticed that I understand a concept only when I try to explain it or build something with it and usually discover halfway through that I didn’t understand it at all. Writing forces me to slow down and untangle ideas, building projects forces me to face all the missing details.

This website is where those two threads come together. One side is a project log: ML, embedded hardware, firmware, sensing pipelines, TinyML deployments and other experiments. The other side is a learning notebook: slow, detailed explanations of the math, ML and signal processing that I’m still trying to make sense of.

Currently building / learning / thinking about

  • TinyML inference on real devices
  • Visualising and cleaning messy sensor data
  • Math foundations behind ML & DSP

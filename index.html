Why I’m building this website

I’ve noticed I understand things far better when I try to explain them — usually discovering halfway through that I understood nothing. Writing helps untangle the chaos in my head, and sharing it keeps me accountable (and mildly embarrassed enough to stay consistent).

I spend most of my time exploring embedded systems, sensing, TinyML & edge AI, data and signal processing, and the math beneath it all. Some things work, some things explode either way, I learn something, and this site is where I document those projects, experiments and ah-ha / oh-no moments.

Currently building / learning / thinking about

  • Hardware + firmware experiments on embedded boards
  • TinyML inference on real devices
  • Visualising and cleaning messy sensor data
  • Math foundations behind ML & DSP

Happy to discuss, collaborate, or learn from your experiences too.
